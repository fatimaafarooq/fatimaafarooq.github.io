I am a Graduate Research Assistant in the LIDS Lab at the University of Texas at Arlington, advised by Prof. Ashraf Aboulnaga. My research focuses on making graph neural networks scale to web-scale knowledge graphs with hundreds of millions of nodes.

I co-developed gHAWK, a GNN training framework that topped the OGB leaderboard for 3 knowledge graph benchmarks. I'm currently exploring relational transformers and LLMs for structured reasoning over relational data.

Before UTA, I spent 2.5 years as a Senior AI Developer at NASTP (Pakistan), building real-time anomaly detection and decision support systems for air defense (800–1,000 tracks/sec). I bring 7+ years of experience across GNNs, NLP, computer vision, geospatial analytics, and systems engineering.
